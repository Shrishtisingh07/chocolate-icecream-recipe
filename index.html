Chocolate Icecream

A little lick of frozen cream every now and then, goes a long, long way.

Ingredients

  • 2 to 2.25 cups whipping cream (30% to 50% fat)
  • ¼ cup whole milk
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • ½ cup sweetened condensed milk
  • ½ cup tightly packed brown sugar – note that you can opt to use regular sugar or equal portions of both regular sugar and brown sugar.

Preparation

  1. Bring the milk, sugar, cocoa, and salt to a simmer in a saucepan.
  2. Whisk ½ of the milk mixture into the yolks, return to the pan, and stir until thickened.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in chopped chocolate.
  4. Refrigerate in a chilled bowl until cold.
  5. Add the remaining ingredients, then freeze in an ice cream maker.
  6. Serve!
